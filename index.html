Find answers to the most common questions about Calineva.

Calineva is a modern multiplayer modification for GTA San Andreas, designed to enhance the original game with multiplayer capabilities. It allows players to enjoy San Andreas with friends in a persistent online world with many unique features not found in other multiplayer mods.

Calineva stands out with its mobile support, cross-platform gameplay between PC and Android, synchronized NPC AI, high player counts, integrated voice chat with noise cancellation, and a developer-friendly API. The mod is built with modern technologies to ensure better performance and stability.

The name "Calineva" (pronounced /klnv/) is a clever fusion that pays homage to the game's setting. In GTA San Andreas, the fictional state features three major cities: Los Santos (based on Los Angeles), San Fierro (based on San Francisco), and Las Venturras (based on Las Vegas). The name combines "Cali" from California (home to LA and SF) and "neva" from Nevada (where Vegas is located), creating a perfect blend that represents the game's diverse landscape and our commitment to bringing life to these iconic locations with the new multiplayer.
